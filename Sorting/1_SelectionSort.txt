========================================================================
Selection Sort
========================================================================
    1) start by initializing 2 pointers ptr1 & ptr2 at 0 & 1 idx
    2) move ptr2 along entire array to search for lowest value
    3) swap lowest value using the 2 ptrs
    4) move ptr1 and ptr2 to idx 1 & 2.
    Repeat step 2 to 4 until entire array sorted
